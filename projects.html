Projects!

Throughout my life, I have participated in many projects.

My Projects:

  • Destination Imagination
  • EMA Leadership
  • WeCanToo Student
  • Volunteering


Destination Imagination: Destination Imagination (DI) is something that I did for 2 consecutive years, with some friends. In this program our creative, and logical skills were put to the test, and we had to go through many competitions. This was a program I enjoyed very much!



EMA Leadership: In this program, I was given the chance to assist in many martial arts classes in the dojo I went to. Every month we attended at training, which taught us the key principles of teaching martial arts. We would be tested after a certain amount of classes assisted in, to progress to the next level of becoming a assistant. Then eventually we would become an instructor!

WeCanToo: WeCanToo is a coding program, that teaches girls coding. I am apart of this program, and I am learning how to code with the help of highschoolers. This program is very informative, and exciting. The knwoledge of coding will help me in the future, and I am grateful that I am apart of this program!

Volunteering: In 2019 I volunteered at an animal shelter, known as East Bay SPCA. There were two reasons for this. The first one was because I love animals, and the second was I wanted to have exposure on how to take care of dogs. I loved this program so much, that my family and I ended up adopting a dog a few months later!

This is my dog Rio!
